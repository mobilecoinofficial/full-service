Enum mc_full_service::service::receipt::ReceiptTransactionStatus

source · 
pub enum ReceiptTransactionStatus {
    TransactionSuccess,
    TransactionPending,
    TxosReceivedAtDifferentBlockIndices,
    InvalidConfirmation,
    DuplicateTxos,
    AmountMismatch(String),
    FailedAmountDecryption,
}

Variants§

§

TransactionSuccess

All Txos are in the ledger at the same block index, and the expected value matches the value of the Txos.

§

TransactionPending

No Txos have landed in the wallet yet.

§

TxosReceivedAtDifferentBlockIndices

All Txos are in the ledger, at different block indices. This indicates the Txos were spent in different transactions, and the receipt is invalid.

§

InvalidConfirmation

Invalid confirmation number.

§

DuplicateTxos

Receipt contains duplicate Txos

§

AmountMismatch(String)

Receipt Amount does not match the Amount in the Txo: {0}

§

FailedAmountDecryption

Failed to decrypt the amount for the given Txo
